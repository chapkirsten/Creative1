Featured Artist

Arctic Monkeys

The featured artist of this week is Arctic Monkeys. This four-member band from Sheffield, England never ceases to amaze audiences with their innovative sound. Their new album, Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino has sat steadily at first in the charts for over four months.

Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino has further proved the staying power of Arctic Monkeys. The '70s meets Serge Gainsbourg' album tells the story of a hotel on the moon. Many say that this is the first time that Arctic Monkeys is dipping their toes into a concept album. The writers here Music Watch would all argue though that the band's previous albums were strongly conceptual in nature.
